About us

We are an integrated tech event organising company in Copenhagen. We specialise in arranging events, conferences, and meetings according to your wishes and needs. Enjoy the benefit of our many years of experience throughout the entire process. Our focus is both on attracting participants to events, as well as on finding partners that are willing to sponsor the events or hold a presentation for an event.

We have an excellent team

Our Professional Team is a knowledge-based group which endeavours to provide best and wide spectrum of event services at one stop. We wish to establish ourself as a destination where all tech events of Copenhagen come together. We have strong foundations by which we wish to achieve robust growth. Our diversified and dedicated team of professionals have expertise in providing supreme event services.

We are centrally located

Our location is in the city centre, which is Copenhagen's bustling heart, a medieval maze of lively streets and attractive squares. Strøget is one of the world's longest pedestrianised streets, crammed full of clothing and houseware shops, cafés and terrace bars from the Rådhuspladsen in the west to Kongens Nytorv in the east.
